How it works

Consultation

We choose the meeting option: online consultation or home visit, if the distance allows. We discuss the function of the space, who will use it and details of furniture and equipment. We will go through color scheme and discuss style. We will determine scope of project, requirements and budget. Take measurements and take photos of existing layout.

Creating concept

We will submit a scope of work with detailed items and ideas discussed in our initial consultation. We will present sketch layout, color palette, list of furniture and accessories.

Purchasing and procurement

Once the design concept is approved, we start ordering the furniture and accessories. We will coordinate and schedule a date for your project installation.

Installation and styling

In this step you can choose the package “Styling” or “Supervision” if you need.

Package “Restyling” is free of charge.
